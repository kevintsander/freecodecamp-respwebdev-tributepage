History of Cincinnati

The Queen City

Timeline of major Cincinnati events:

  • 1788 - Losantiville settled
  • 1790 - Losantiville renamed Cincinnati
  • 1869 - Cincinnati Reds baseball team founded - first professional team
  • 1871 - Tyler Davidson Fountain dedicated
  • 1875 - Cincinnati Zoo opened
  • 1878 - Music Hall Built
  • 1914 - Martha, last passenger pigeon, dies at Cincinnati Zoo
  • 1933 - Union Terminal opened
  • 1966 - Cincinnati Bengals football team founded
  • 1979 - "The Who" concert disaster
  • 2001 - Cincinnati riots after police shooting
  • 2018 - FC Cincinnati soccer team founded

“When the end of the world comes, I want to be in Cincinnati because it's always 20 years behind the times.” - Mark Twain
